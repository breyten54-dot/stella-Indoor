Privacy Policy

Stella Indoor Sports Hub, Durban, South Africa · Applies to the Stella Indoor booking app, the Stella Admin app, and the websites stella-indoor.web.app and stella-indoor-admin.web.app · Last updated: 10 July 2026

What we collect

  • Account details: your name, email address and password (stored as a secure hash) when you create an account.
  • Booking details: the courts, dates and times you book, optional team name, and a contact phone number you provide with a booking.
  • Notifications: if you enable push notifications, a push subscription token for your device.

What we use it for

  • Creating and managing your court bookings.
  • Sending booking confirmations, reminders and cancellation notices by email and (if enabled) push notification.
  • Letting facility staff manage the booking calendar.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your information.
  • We do not share your information with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not use advertising or advertising trackers.

Where your data lives

Data is stored on Google Firebase (authentication, database and hosting) and transactional emails are sent via Brevo. Both process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy commitments. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

Your rights (POPIA)

Under South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. Email us at stellasportshub@gmail.com and we will action your request. Deleting your account removes your personal details from active systems; booking records required for business administration may be retained in anonymised form.
